The upper left pane shows the hierarchal order of commits and how they relate to the various branches. The upper right pane shows details such as the date the action was administered. The window in the middle (in the upper half) shows the author for that action. Below these details SHA1 which is a unique ID for that action is displayed. The bottom left pane shows details of the action. In the case of commits we see the corresponding changelog and the difference in files. The bottom right pane shows the files impacted by the commit/action.
